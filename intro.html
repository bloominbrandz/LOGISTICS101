📖 About This Video

This introductory video from Daily Logistics explains the fundamental definition of logistics and introduces the core framework known as the "7 Rights of Logistics" — the essential principles that govern how goods and services move efficiently through the supply chain.

You'll discover how logistics has evolved from ancient trade routes to modern digital supply networks, and why it remains the hidden engine of global commerce.

7️⃣ The 7 Rights of Logistics

The video explains the golden rule of logistics: delivering the right product, in the right quantity and right condition, to the right place at the right time, for the right customer, at the right cost.

1

Right Product

Exactly what the customer ordered

2

Right Quantity

Accurate amount, no more no less

3

Right Condition

Undamaged and fit for use

4

Right Place

Delivered to correct destination

5

Right Time

When the customer needs it

6

Right Customer

To the intended recipient

7

Right Cost

At the most efficient price point to maintain profitability and competitiveness

🎯 Learning Objectives

After watching this video, you should be able to:

  • Define logistics and distinguish it from supply chain management
  • Explain the historical evolution of logistics from ancient to modern times
  • Identify and describe each of the 7 Rights of Logistics
  • Recognize how logistics creates value in the global economy
  • Apply the 7 Rights framework to evaluate real-world logistics scenarios
