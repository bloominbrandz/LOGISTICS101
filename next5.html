Module 06: Accessorials

Protecting the Margin: TONU, Detention, & Layovers

Standard: $150-$250

TONU

Truck Order Not Used. Paid when a shipper cancels a load after the driver has already been dispatched.

Standard: $50/hr

Detention

Wait Time. Usually kicks in after 2 hours of "Free Time" at the dock. Requires signed in/out times.

Standard: $250-$400

Layover

24-Hour Delay. Paid when a driver is forced to stay overnight because the shipper/receiver cannot load/unload.

The Detention Formula

The Scenario:

  • Appt Time: 08:00
  • Check-Out: 13:00
  • Total Time: 5 Hours

The Calculation:

5 hrs - 2 hrs Free = 3 Billable

Total: 3 x $50 = $150

The Payables List

Lumper

Third-party laborers at warehouses who unload trailers. Requires a "Lumper Receipt" for reimbursement.

Deadhead

Miles driven with an empty trailer to get to a load. High deadhead usually requires a higher rate.

Scale Fee

Small fee ($12-$15) paid to verify the truck is not over the 80,000 lbs legal limit.

Driver Assist

When a driver has to physically help move boxes. Usually costs an extra $75-$150.
