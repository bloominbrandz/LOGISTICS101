Module 03: Carrier Sales

Mastering the SELL Framework & Market Ratios

01. DAT Load-to-Truck Ratios

Market equilibrium is 2.5 loads per truck. Use these ratios to determine your negotiation leverage:

0.5 - 1.5

Shipper Market

Trucks are everywhere. You dictate the price. Push for 15-20% below spot.

1.5 - 3.0

Balanced Market

Standard equilibrium. Use service history and QuickPay as leverage.

3.0+

Carrier Market

Trucks are scarce. Carriers hold the power. Pay the premium or lose the load.

Sales Glossary

QuickPay

Paying the carrier in 24-48 hours (for a 2-5% fee) instead of the standard 30 days.

Deadhead

Distance a driver travels with an empty trailer to reach a pickup location.

Headhaul

High-demand lanes out of a major market. Rates are usually higher here.

Backhaul

Lanes going back toward a carrier's home base. Rates are often lower to keep the truck moving.

Anchoring

The psychological tactic of starting a negotiation with a very low (or high) price to set the baseline.
