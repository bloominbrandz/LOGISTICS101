Module 04: Track & Trace

The 4-Call Framework & Cargo Security Protocols

01. The Valoroo 4-Call System

Call 1: Pre-Pickup

2-4 Hours Before Pickup

Verify driver location, HOS hours, and if the trailer is clean/dry.

Call 2: In-Transit

Every 6-8 Hours

Verify GPS location vs. ETA. Proactively flag weather or traffic delays.

Call 3: Pre-Arrival

2 Hours Before Delivery

Confirm driver is on target. Call the receiver to ensure they are ready.

Call 4: Post-Delivery

Immediately After Delivery

Confirm "Clean POD" with no damage. Collect the paperwork via app.

🚨 Dark Truck Protocol

When GPS pings stop and a driver goes "ghost," time is your enemy. Follow this escalation:

  • 15 Min: Double-call driver and text "URGENT LOCATION UPDATE NEEDED."
  • 30 Min: Call carrier dispatch and demand an ELD screenshot.
  • 45 Min: Alert the shipper of a "Potential Technical Issue" (Soft Warning).
  • 60 Min: Initiate cargo theft protocol; notify authorities for high-value loads.

Visibility Glossary

Geofencing

A virtual "fence" around a route. If the truck leaves the fence, an automatic alert is triggered.

Spoofing

When a driver uses an app to fake their GPS location. Common in cargo theft scams.

MacroPoint

A popular 3rd-party tracking software that pings a driver's phone location automatically.

Welfare Check

Asking local police to find a truck's location to ensure the driver is safe and cargo is secure.
