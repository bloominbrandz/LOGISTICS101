Breakdowns, Rejections, and Dark Truck Protocols

🚨 Dark Truck: The 60-Minute Countdown

1
0-15 Minutes: Blitz

Call driver 3x. Call carrier dispatch. Check ELD for a "Pings" vs "Actuals" mismatch.

2
15-45 Minutes: The Soft Alert

Notify the Shipper. "We have a tracking gap." Ask if they've had contact. Start looking for backup trucks.

3
45-60 Minutes: Law Enforcement

File a police report in the last known city. Notify insurance. Declare an active cargo emergency.

The Rejection Matrix

Timing Legal Status Your Move

At Shipper Breach of Contract Demand TONU. Find immediate replacement.
Mid-Transit Conversion / Theft Call Police Immediately.

Crisis Glossary

Transload

Moving freight from one truck to another mid-transit due to breakdown or rejection.

Subrogation

The legal right of an insurance company to "step into the shoes" of the broker to sue a carrier for damages.

Conversion

The legal term for when a carrier refuses to deliver a load and effectively "steals" possession of it.

Reefer Breakdown

When the cooling unit on a trailer fails. In food/pharma, this is an automatic "Total Loss" emergency.
