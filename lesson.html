01. The Four Pillars

Shipper

The company with the product. They hire the broker and provide the BOL.

Broker

The middleman (Mode Global). Manages the freight without owning the trucks.

Carrier

The trucking company (Unis). Owns the assets and moves the physical goods.

Consignee

The receiver. They sign the POD to prove the cargo arrived safely.

Logistics Bible

BOL

Bill of Lading. The master contract. If it's not on the BOL, it didn't happen.

POD

Proof of Delivery. The signature at the end. Without this, the carrier doesn't get paid.

TONU

Truck Order Not Used. The penalty fee ($150-$250) paid to a carrier if you cancel a load last minute.

MC#

Motor Carrier Number. A company's fingerprint with the government. Used to check for fraud.

HOS

Hours of Service. The law. Drivers can only drive 11 hours before they MUST rest.
