Basic Logistics Concepts

Building the foundation for strategic supply chain management.

Learning Outcomes

By the end of this module, you will be equipped to:

  • Analyze the core principles of modern logistics.
  • Outline the critical importance of logistics in the global economy.
  • Evaluate supply chain and value network performance metrics.
  • Discuss the integration of value chains and networks.
  • Describe strategies for maintaining a sustainable competitive advantage.
  • Identify high-growth career opportunities in the logistics sector.

Why this Module?

Logistics is the "hidden engine" of the world. In this opening module, we move beyond simple transportation to understand how value is created through the seamless flow of goods, information, and capital.
